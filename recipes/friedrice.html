Fried Rice

Delicious and versatile fried rice. Great for clearing out leftover ingredients from your fridge and freezer.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 lb protein
  • 1/2 cup veggetables
  • 2 cups cold rice (approximate)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Beat 2 eggs in a bowl and fry over medium low heat in hot pan with a dash of oil (do not overcook;egg should still look wet). Set aside.
  2. Add 2 tsp of oil to same pan and turn up heat to medium high to fry protein.
  3. Once protein has browned, add vegetables and fry for about 1 minutes.
  4. Add in cold cooked rice. Break clumps apart with spatula, add a dash of water to loosen up hard clumps.
  5. Toss in eggs and combine, then add 1 tsp soy sauce and sesame oil.
  6. Salt to taste, make sure to combine thoroughly.
