Mapo Tofu

Traditional Sichuan tofu dish. Addictive numbing spicey sauce paired with bouncy soft tofu to balance out the heat.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs whole sichuan peppercorns
  • 7 dried chili peppers, chopped
  • 2 tbs oil
  • 1 tbs minced ginger
  • 1 tbs minced garlic
  • 1/4 lb ground pork
  • 2 tbs doubanjiang
  • 14oz medium firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 tbs cornstarch
  • 2 cups + 1/4 cup water

Steps

  1. Toast 1 tbs whole sichuan peppercorns until fragrant, set aside.
  2. Heat 2 tbs of oil over medium heat with chopped chili peppers.
  3. Add in minced ginger and garlic until golden and fragrant.
  4. Turn up heat to medium high and add 1/4 lb ground pork. Cook until color changes.
  5. Add 2 tbs doubanjiang, stir fry with ground pork until coated.
  6. Add cubed tofu and stir fry for another minute, then add 2 cups water.
  7. When water comes to boil, lower heat to simmer and make cornstarch slurry with remaining 1/4 cup water.
  8. Slowly mix cornstarch slurry into simmering tofu until sauce thickens to your liking.
  9. Serve with plain white rice or noodles.
