Miso Soup

Savory miso soup for cold nights to comfort the soul. Umami fish based broth with complex miso, soft sweet tofu and antioxidant rich seaweed.

Ingredients

  • 5g kombu seaweed
  • 3 dried anchovies or 1 tbs hondashi
  • 4 cups water
  • 7oz silken tofu
  • 2 tbs miso paste
  • 2 tsp dried seaweed
  • 1/2 tsp sake or rice wine
