Miso Soup

Savory miso soup for cold nights to comfort the soul. Umami fish based broth with complex miso, soft sweet tofu and antioxidant rich seaweed.

Ingredients

  • 5g kombu seaweed
  • 3 dried anchovies or 1 tbs hondashi
  • 4 cups water
  • 7oz silken tofu
  • 2 tbs miso paste
  • 2 tsp dried seaweed
  • 1/2 tsp sake or rice wine
  • 1 scallion
    •

    Steps

    1. Combine 5 grams of kombu seaweed and 3 dried anchovies (or 1 tbs hondashi) with 4 cups of water and bring to a boil over medium heat.
    2. Once water is boiling, set timer for 20 minutes and let broth reduce (do not cover with lid).
    3. While broth simmers, cube 7oz of silken tofu and thinly slice scallions.
    4. After 20 minutes, straing out seaweed and anchovies and add cubed tofu.
    5. When soup comes to boil again, lower heat and add 2 tbs of miso paste, stirring until dissolved.
    6. Once miso paste is completely dissolved, add 1 tsp dried seaweed and 1 tsp sake (or rice wine).
    7. Serve with thinly sliced scallions.
